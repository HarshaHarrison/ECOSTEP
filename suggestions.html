🌿 Tips to Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

🚶 Walk or Cycle

Walking or biking reduces emissions and keeps you healthy!

🛍️ Use Reusable Bags

Avoid plastic. Carry cloth bags for shopping.

💡 Switch Off Lights

Turn off lights when not in use to save electricity.

🌞 Use Solar Power

Installing solar panels reduces dependency on fossil fuels.

🚿 Take Shorter Showers

Reducing shower time saves water and energy.

🍃 Eat Local & Plant-Based

Support local farmers and reduce food miles. Eating more plants helps too!

📦 Recycle Properly

Separate waste and recycle plastic, paper, and metal correctly.

🌳 Plant a Tree

Trees absorb CO₂ and provide oxygen. Great for the planet!

🔌 Unplug Devices

Electronics still use power when idle. Unplug to save energy.

🧺 Wash in Cold Water

Washing clothes in cold water saves energy and works just as well.
